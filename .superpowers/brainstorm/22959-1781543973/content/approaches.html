Three page structure approaches

Clean & Confident visual direction — which narrative arc fits best?

Bundle-First
① HERO — "Everything you need to use AI, bundled." + Sign up free
② BUNDLE BREAKDOWN — 3 pills: AI Accounts · SIM/eSIM · API Router
③ PAIN vs SOLUTION — Before/After comparison (problem cards)
④ MODEL COVERAGE — Scrolling logos (OpenAI, Claude, Gemini…)
⑤ MODEL LAB SPOTLIGHT — "Try it free — sign up, get credits"
⑥ PRICING TEASER — Plans overview
⑦ FINAL CTA

A — Bundle-First

Opens by naming all three product components immediately. Best when visitors arrive knowing they have a problem but don't know what the solution looks like. Direct and informative.

Pros

  • Crystal clear what you're buying
  • No guessing game

Cons

  • Slightly utilitarian — less narrative pull
Story-Led ★ Recommended
① HERO — "Global AI without the headache." + CTA pair
② MODEL LOGOS — Trust strip: OpenAI · Claude · Gemini · more
③ PROBLEM — 3 pain cards (accounts / payment / network)
④ SOLUTION BUNDLE — "We handle all three." Feature grid
⑤ MODEL LAB — Full-width showcase + "New users get free credits"
⑥ PLANS — Pricing overview
⑦ FINAL CTA

B — Story-Led ★

Leads with the relatable pain (accounts, payment, network friction), then positions Nyquiste as the clean answer. Model Lab arrives once the visitor understands the product. The "free credits" nudge lands at exactly the right moment.

Pros

  • Emotional resonance before the ask
  • Model Lab feels earned, not forced
  • Natural conversion arc

Cons

  • Slightly longer scroll before the offer
Model Lab-Led
① HERO — "Compare any AI model, free." + sign up CTA
② MODEL LAB DEMO — Animated/screenshot preview, sign-up gate
③ "AND THAT'S JUST THE START" — Bundle components revealed
④ AI ACCOUNTS — What's included, which providers
⑤ SIM/eSIM — Global connectivity explained
⑥ PLANS
⑦ CTA

C — Model Lab-Led

Model Lab is the headline product. Leads with the interactive hook immediately, then reveals the rest of the bundle below. Bets that the demo sells better than any description.

Pros

  • Highest activation intent at top
  • Demo does the persuading

Cons

  • Undersells the full bundle upfront
  • Visitors may not understand what they're signing up for
